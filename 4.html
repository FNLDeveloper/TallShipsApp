Rules and Regulations

Please follow our rules and regulations:

*All attending public will be screened prior to entry, including their personal belongings.

*No large bags, backpacks, or large coolers will be permitted.

*No weapons, of any kind! This includes but is not limited to firearms and knives.

*No open containers of liquid or outside food. Baby food and formula must be contained in plastic containers. Glass is not permitted. A small cooler, no larger than five quarts may be brought in for baby food or formula and medicine only.

*Everyone must have a credential or a paid ticket to get access to the event.


Thank You!
